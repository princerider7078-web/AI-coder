GROWPLANTS
Business Requirement Document (BRD)
Version 1.0
Status: Business Scope Freeze
1. Executive Summary

GROWPLANTS ek location-based eCommerce aur Gardening Service Platform hoga jo users ko plants, planters aur gardening services ek hi platform par provide karega.

Platform ka initial launch Sonipat (Haryana) mein hoga aur future mein multi-city expansion support karega.

System customers ko products purchase karne, gardening services book karne aur future AI-powered plant assistance provide karne ke liye design kiya jayega.

2. Business Vision

India ka trusted digital gardening ecosystem banana.

Jahan user:

Plants purchase kar sake
Planters purchase kar sake
Gardening products kharid sake
Gardening experts hire kar sake
Plant care assistance le sake

Sab ek single platform par.

3. Mission Statement
