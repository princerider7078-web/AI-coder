    80→    80→1.3 ASSUMPTIONS
    81→    81→---------------
    82→    82→1. Platform launches in Sonipat, Haryana first.
    83→    83→2. Business owner manages inventory directly (no multi-vendor product marketplace).
    84→    84→3. Service providers are third-party freelancers onboarded by admin.
    85→    85→4. Payment gateway: Razorpay (India-first, supports UPI, cards, wallets).
    86→    86→5. Delivery is managed in-house or via local courier for Phase 1.
    87→    87→6. Mobile app: React Native (single codebase, Android + iOS).
    88→    88→7. Backend: Node.js + Express or NestJS (developer's choice, must be RESTful).
    89→    89→8. Database: PostgreSQL (primary) + Redis (caching/sessions).
    90→    90→9. File storage: AWS S3 or Cloudflare R2.
    91→    91→10. All prices in Indian Rupees (INR).
    92→    92→11. Language: Hindi + English bilingual support (UI labels bilingual).
    93→    93→12. Guest checkout is NOT allowed — account required for all purchases.

   107→   107→SECTION 02 — PRODUCT VISION & SCOPE
   108→   108→================================================================================
   109→   109→
   110→   110→2.1 PRODUCT OVERVIEW
   111→   111→---------------------
   112→   112→GROWPLANTS is a location-based eCommerce and Service Marketplace platform for
   113→   113→the gardening ecosystem in India. It combines:
   114→   114→
   115→   115→  PILLAR 1: PRODUCT STORE
   116→   116→    → Online store for plants, planters, gardening supplies.
   117→   117→    → Owner-managed inventory.
   118→   118→    → Delivery within serviceable pincodes.
   119→   119→
   120→   120→  PILLAR 2: SERVICE MARKETPLACE
   121→   121→    → Customers can book gardening services.
   122→   122→    → Services fulfilled by verified service providers.
   123→   123→    → Admin approves all service providers.
   124→   124→    → Commission-based revenue model.
   125→   125→
   126→   126→  PILLAR 3: ADMIN CONTROL CENTER
   127→   127→    → Full platform management by business owner/admin.
   128→   128→    → Order, booking, inventory, provider, and customer management.
   129→   129→
   130→   130→2.2 PHASE SCOPE
   131→   131→----------------
   132→   132→PHASE 1 (MVP — 0-6 months):
   133→   133→  - Customer Web App + Mobile App
   134→   134→  - Product Catalog (Plants, Planters, Gardening Products)
   135→   135→  - Cart + Checkout + Payment
   136→   136→  - Order Management
   137→   137→  - Service Booking (basic)
   138→   138→  - Service Provider Panel (basic)
   139→   139→  - Admin Panel (full)
   140→   140→  - Inventory Management
   141→   141→  - Review & Rating
   142→   142→  - Notification System (Email + Push + SMS)
   143→   143→  - Sonipat city only
   144→   144→
   145→   145→PHASE 2 (6-18 months):
   146→   146→  - Multi-city support (Haryana)
   147→   147→  - Advanced analytics dashboard
   148→   148→  - Featured listings for service providers
   149→   149→  - Vendor subscription model
   150→   150→
   151→   151→PHASE 3 (18-36 months):
   152→   152→  - National expansion
   153→   153→  - AI recommendation engine
   154→   154→  - AI plant doctor
   155→   155→  - AR plant preview
   156→   156→  - Advanced B2B features
   157→   157→
   158→   158→2.3 PLATFORM TYPES
   159→   159→-------------------
